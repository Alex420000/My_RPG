Welcome in World of Warcraft Not Classic !
It's a little RPG game, your goal is to complete the main quest.
You can move with Up/Right/Down/Left arrows, interact with E,
attack with SPACE.
You have lots of menu like skill tree with K, pause menu with M,
inventory with I.
You have lots of options to configure the game like sound, screen size...
You can edit the map with our editor.

Complete the quest, kill ennemies, and begin the savior of WoW Not Classic !
